About Us

Our orphanage home is dedicated to providing a safe and nurturing environment for children who have lost parental care or are facing hardship. Established in 2010, we have grown from a small community initiative into a fully licensed facility that supports over 120 children each year.

Our Mission

To empower vulnerable children by meeting their basic needs, fostering personal development, and creating opportunities for a brighter future.

Our Vision

We envision a world where every child experiences love, education, and the chance to reach their full potential.

What We Offer

  • Safe Shelter: Comfortable living spaces with round-the-clock care.
  • Education: Access to primary and secondary schooling, with tutoring and vocational training.
  • Health & Wellbeing: Regular medical checkups, nutritious meals, and mental health support.
  • Life Skills: Programs in hygiene, financial literacy, and arts to prepare children for independence.

How You Can Help

Support comes through donations, volunteering, or spreading awareness. Every contribution helps us continue this essential work.
