Our Services

Basic Support Services

  • Shelter and Accommodation
  • Educational Programs
  • Health and Nutrition Support
  • Recreational Activities

Specialized Care Services

  • Mental Health & Trauma Counseling – specialized therapy for children suffering from war, natural disasters or abuse.
  • Child Protection & Advocacy – addressing global issues like child trafficking, exploitation, and abuse by working with law enforcement and NGOs.

Emergency & Crisis Response

  • Disaster Response & Relief – emergency shelter and care for orphans affected by earthquakes, floods, or conflicts worldwide.

Community Support

  • Family Reintegration Services – support for reconnecting children with extended family or safe foster placements when possible.
  • Community Empowerment – training and resources to help local communities prevent orphanhood through poverty alleviation and health education.
